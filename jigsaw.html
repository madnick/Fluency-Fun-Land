Jigsaw

Jigsaw exercises provide you and your child with a collection of different pictures to reconstruct. Jigsaw puzzles are a great exercise to aid the development of reasoning, sequence and problem solving skills. Jigsaws are also a great opportunity for your child to further develop their hand-eye coordination and spatial awareness skills.

Instructions

There are 5 different jigsaw pictures to choose from on the menu. Click and drag to assemble the pieces of the jigsaw according to the background preview of the completed image. Pieces that are in the correct place will attach themselves to other pieces already there.
